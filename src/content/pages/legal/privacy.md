# Privacy Policy

*Effective date: May 2026. Last updated: May 2026.*

---

## What This Policy Covers

This Privacy Policy explains how FundingCompass (fundingcompass.guide) collects, uses, and protects information about visitors to this website. It covers our use of Google Analytics 4 (GA4), cookies, and any voluntary information you provide via the contact form.

FundingCompass does not sell personal data. We do not run advertising that uses personal data for targeting. We do not have advertising relationships that require data sharing.

---

## Information We Collect Automatically

**Google Analytics 4 (GA4).** We use GA4 (property ID: G-LDN1LRELFK) to understand how visitors use this site — which pages are visited, how long they stay, and how they navigate. GA4 collects:

- Pages visited and time spent
- Referring website or search term (if any)
- General geographic location (country, region — not precise location)
- Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet) and browser type
- Session and user counts

GA4 does not collect your name, email address, or any directly identifying information unless you voluntarily provide it. GA4 data is processed by Google under Google's Privacy Policy.

**Cookies.** GA4 uses first-party cookies to distinguish sessions and users. These cookies do not identify you personally. At launch, FundingCompass uses analytics cookies only — no advertising, retargeting, or third-party marketing cookies are set.

Cookie banner: On first visit, we present a cookie consent notice. Analytics cookies are set only after consent is given for visitors from the EEA, UK, and other regions where consent is legally required.

---

## Information You Provide Voluntarily

**Contact form.** If you contact us via the contact form, we collect your name, email address, and the content of your message. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not add contact form submissions to marketing lists.

**Email contact.** If you email hello@fundingcompass.guide, we collect the information in your email for the purpose of responding to you.

## How We Use Your Information

| Data | Purpose | Legal Basis (GDPR) |
|---|---|---|
| GA4 analytics data | Understanding site usage; improving content | Legitimate interest (with consent where required) |
| Contact form data | Responding to your inquiry | Contract performance / legitimate interest |

We do not use your data for:
- Targeted advertising
- Third-party data sharing for marketing
- Automated decision-making
- Profiling

---

## Your Rights

**GDPR (EEA and UK residents):** You have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data; to object to processing; and to withdraw consent for cookie-based analytics. Contact us at hello@fundingcompass.guide to exercise these rights.

**CCPA (California residents):** You have the right to know what personal information we collect, to delete it, and to opt out of its sale (we do not sell personal data). Contact us at hello@fundingcompass.guide to exercise CCPA rights.

**Opt-out of GA4:** You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking across all websites using the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

---

## Data Retention

GA4 data is retained for 14 months within Google Analytics (the minimum configurable period). Contact form data is retained for 12 months or until the relevant inquiry is resolved, whichever is shorter.
